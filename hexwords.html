Hex Words

Find the English hex() words. You know, the strings of hex digits that appear to be English words.

For example...

  • The word 'bee' in hex is 3054 in decimal.
  • The word 'face' in hex is 64206 in decimal.
  • The word 'decade' in hex is 14600926 in decimal.

Forget about the '0x' prefix. That's computer stuff! http://stackoverflow.com/questions/2670639/why-are-hexadecimal-prefixed-as-0x

So, what is the largest hex word in English? (hint: You can eat some of them.)
